Key Differences

Aadhaar-Linked Account

  • ✗ Aadhaar registered with bank
  • ✗ NOT connected to DBT system
  • ✗ Cannot receive scholarships
  • ✗ Requires additional seeding

DBT-Enabled Seeded Account

  • ✓ Aadhaar registered with bank
  • ✓ Connected to DBT system
  • ✓ Can receive scholarships directly
  • ✓ Ready for government benefits

How to Seed Your Aadhaar

1

Gather Documents

Original Aadhaar card and bank passbook

2

Visit Bank Branch

Go to your account holding bank branch

3

Request Seeding

Ask for DBT Aadhaar Seeding Service

4

Verification

Bank verifies and submits to NPCI

5

Confirm Status

Check online after 2-3 days
